# Mine-Safety-Incident-Classification
Classification related to health and safety in the mining industry
**Problem Statement**
Mining operations face various health and safety risks, and accurately classifying incidents is crucial for identifying potential hazards, assessing risks, and implementing preventive measures. The challenge in this hackathon is to build a model capable of accurately classifying incidents based on their descriptions. Additionally, participants are required to provide a framework for evaluating risk from each incident and identifying potential focus areas for improvement.
